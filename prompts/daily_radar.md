# Daily Radar Prompt

Use this prompt to track one important AI or tech trend each day in 10 to 15 minutes.

```text
Help me choose one high-signal AI or technology topic for today's radar note.

Requirements:
- pick only 1 topic
- prefer something recent and important
- keep the reading time under 15 minutes
- prioritize signal over hype

Possible categories:
- frontier AI models
- LLM training and evaluation
- agents and tool use
- inference and hardware
- developer tooling
- AI safety and security
- cloud platform changes
- semiconductors

For today's topic, give me:
1. the topic title
2. the source name
3. the link
4. a short summary
5. why it matters
6. whether it affects my learning roadmap
7. 1 follow-up term to study later

Then format the output so I can save it directly into today's radar note.
```
