# Progress Report Prompt

Use this when you want the AI to generate a progress report from the repo artifacts.

```text
Act as my study program reviewer.

Use my repository artifacts to create a short progress report:
- roadmap status
- this week's momentum
- what I can likely explain
- what I still do not understand well
- implementation progress
- whether I am moving too fast or too slowly
- the best next topic

Be honest and specific.
Call out weak fundamentals clearly.
Do not praise progress unless it is tied to evidence in the logs or code.
```
