# End of Day Prompt

Use this after finishing the session.

```text
Here is what I studied today:

[PASTE NOTES, CODE SUMMARY, QUESTIONS, AND RESULTS]

Now do the following:
1. Summarize what I completed.
2. Tell me what I understood well.
3. Identify weak points or misconceptions.
4. Suggest what should go into today's daily log.
5. Update my weekly goal status.
6. Create tomorrow's 3-hour plan.
7. Give me 5 review questions from today's material.
```
